Popular Apple Varieties

Himachal Pradesh is known for growing several world-class apple varieties that are prized for their taste and quality.

Red Delicious

A popular sweet and juicy variety with a bright red color. Known for its distinctive heart shape and five bottom lobes.

Golden Delicious

Sweet, soft, and tender with a golden yellow color. Excellent for both fresh eating and cooking applications.

Royal Gala

Known for its fine texture and balanced sweetness. Features a distinctive striped red pattern on a pale yellow background.
